ABOUT ISRO
// INDIAN SPACE RESEARCH ORGANISATION · EST. 1969

India's national space agency operates 50+ satellites providing Earth observation, weather data, and disaster intelligence — all powered by live APIs from OpenWeather and NASA FIRMS.

  • CARTOSAT series delivers sub-meter imagery for real-time disaster mapping.
  • INSAT-3D/3DR monitors cyclones, cloud cover and rainfall over all of India.
  • ISRO data helped evacuate 10 million people before Cyclone Phailin (2013).
  • Indian Knowledge System enhances satellite models with centuries of local ecological wisdom.
  • 104 satellites launched in a single mission — world record in 2017.
"Space Courage means using bold technology to protect our planet — India's gift to the world."
